About Our College

Vani Pre-University College, Belthangady, One of the reputed Pre-University Colleges in Belthangady - Karnataka, was established in the year of 2004 by Gowdara Yane Okkaliga Seva Sangha (R) with the vision of providing Quality Education to the rural parts of Belthangady Taluk - Karnataka. Today the Institute has been identified as a centre for excellence in imparting Pre University education to aspiring students from all over the country and is bringing out best academic performance from the students. All the morden facilities required for quiality education is provided with in the campus.
