Eligibility for Ist P.U.C

  • A pass in the Karnataka SSLC Examination or an equivalent examination.
  • Students who have passed 10th/CBSE/ICSE are also eligible.
  • At the time of the interview the applicant should produce S.S.L.C. Provisional Marks card signed by the Headmaster.
  • S.S.L.C. Marks card in Original with two xerox copies of the same.
  • Original Transfer Certificate with one xerox copies.
  • Conduct Certificate with one xerox copy.
  • A pass in the Karnataka SSLC Examination or an equivalent examination.
  • Passport and 2 stamp size Photographs.
  • Eligibility Certificates (only for outsiders).
  • SSLC Exam Hall Ticket xerox
  • The Parent or Guardian shall invaribaly accompany the candidate at the time of admission.

Admission & Rules

  • Admission to the first year Pre-University course is open to the students who have passed the Karnataka S.S.L.C. examination or an equivalent examination.
  • Application in the Prescribed form filled by the candidate must be submitted to the college office in advance.
  • Candidates from other states should produce provisional eligibility certificate
  • Admission to the college is for one academic year only. All students shall seek re-admission after the first year.
